Langzhe Gu (顾朗哲)

I'm an undergraduate at Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. I'm honored to be advised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang at PKU HMI Lab.

Previously, I was a research intern at IIIS and Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute, where I am fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Huazhe Xu.

Currently, I'm fortunate to be a visiting student intern at RoboTouch Lab, advised by Prof. Wenzhen Yuan.

My research interests include robot navigation, tactile sensing and full-stack robot design. I'm passionate about exploring the intersection of robotics and AI, aiming to develop intelligent systems that can seamlessly interact with and adapt to their environments.
